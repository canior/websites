Academy Tutor

Location: Remote, Canada

Contract Type: Full-Time / Contract

Salary: $30 - $35/hour

About Learn2.Online: At Learn2.Online, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality online tutoring services to students in various academic disciplines. Our approach focuses on personalized, engaging, and effective learning experiences, utilizing the latest digital tools.

Job Summary: We are seeking experienced and enthusiastic Academy Tutors to join our team on a contractual basis. The role involves delivering online tutoring in your area of expertise, providing personalized educational support, and contributing to our students' academic success.

Key Responsibilities: Provide online tutoring sessions to students in your area of specialization. Create tailored lesson plans that address individual student needs and learning objectives. Foster a positive and supportive learning environment. Regularly assess and report on student progress. Collaborate with other educators and staff to enhance teaching methodologies. Stay updated with current educational trends and practices in your subject area.

Qualifications: Diploma or degree in Education, Communication, Business Administration, or a related field. Proven experience as a tutor or educator, with a strong track record of student success. Excellent command of the subject matter and current curriculum standards. Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with an ability to engage students effectively. Familiarity with online teaching tools and platforms. Self-motivated, organized, and able to work independently.

What We Offer:

  • Flexible scheduling to fit your lifestyle and commitments.
  • A dynamic, supportive, and collaborative work environment.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development in the field of online education.
  • Access to a wide range of educational resources and tools.
  • Competitive compensation based on experience and qualifications.
